This Privacy Policy explains how [App Name] ("we", "the app") handles information when you use it. The app is a casual puzzle game monetized solely through advertising. We do not sell in-app purchases and we do not ask you to create an account.

1. Information we collect

  • Stored only on your device (never sent to us): game progress, high score, selected language and skin, sound settings. This lives in your device's local storage and is removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Collected automatically by our advertising provider (Google AdMob): device and app identifiers (such as the Advertising Identifier — IDFA on iOS / AAID on Android), approximate (non-precise) location derived from IP address, device type and OS, and ad-interaction/diagnostic data. This is used to serve and measure ads.

We ourselves do not operate servers that collect your personal data; the data above is processed by Google as our advertising partner.

2. How the information is used

  • To display advertising (rewarded videos and interstitials) and, where you consent, personalized ads.
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • To save your game progress and preferences locally on your device.

3. Advertising, consent & your choices

  • Google AdMob is our advertising partner. Its handling of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and “How Google uses information”: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • EU/EEA/UK (GDPR): on first launch we show a consent message (Google UMP) letting you accept or decline personalized ads. You can change your choice anytime from the in-app privacy options.
  • iOS App Tracking Transparency: we ask for your permission before tracking. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): you may opt out of the "sale"/"sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising by declining consent as above.

4. Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under [13 / the age required in your region]. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided such information, contact us and we will delete it.

5. Data retention

On-device data persists until you clear it or uninstall the app. Data processed by Google for advertising is retained per Google's own policies.

6. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at [you@example.com].

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above will reflect the latest revision.

8. Contact
